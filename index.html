About Us

At Maweshi Mandi, we are passionate about livestock and dedicated to providing valuable information to farmers and enthusiasts alike. Our mission is to promote sustainable livestock farming practices and support the agricultural community. We believe in the importance of education and strive to share our knowledge on cow and goat care, breeding, and farming techniques to help our audience succeed in their livestock endeavors.
